About Me

Hi, I'm Stefan, a 24-year-old passionate frontend developer in the making!

I discovered coding out of curiosity, but it quickly turned into a true passion. I love building modern, user-friendly websites and I’m always eager to learn new technologies and push my limits.

I enjoy both teamwork and independent work – it all depends on the people and the project. I truly value patience, support, and clear communication in every collaboration.

My long-term goal is to join a company that appreciates flexible work, creative thinking, and constant learning. I want to build a career I’m proud of, while also enjoying the journey and sharing my knowledge along the way.
